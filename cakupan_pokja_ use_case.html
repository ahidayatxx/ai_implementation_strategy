📅 Framework 4-Fase Strategis

Fase 1: Foundation Building (2025-2026)

PRISMA-AI deployment, standardisasi protokol, basic AI literacy programs

📊 Target: 100+ use cases, 500+ AI practitioners certified

Fase 2: Scale-Up Integration (2027-2028)

15-25 operational AI implementations, cross-sector integration

📊 Target: 70% K/L dengan operational AI, 80% citizen satisfaction

Fase 3: Optimization Leadership (2029-2030)

40-50 mature use cases, regional leadership positioning

📊 Target: Top 20 Global AI Readiness Index, 90% data interoperability

Fase 4: Sustained Excellence (2031-2035)

100+ operational use cases, ASEAN standards leadership

📊 Target: 3% GDP contribution, Indonesian standards adoption
